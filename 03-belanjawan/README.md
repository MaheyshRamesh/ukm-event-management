# 03 — Budgeting (Belanjawan Terperinci)

Every program needs a detailed budget (Belanjawan Terperinci) as part of its Kertas Kerja. This section provides templates and real examples.

---

## Budget Structure

A compliant UKM belanjawan has two sections:

### A. Anggaran Pendapatan (Estimated Revenue)
Lists all funding sources and amounts:

| No. | Source | Amount (RM) |
|-----|--------|-------------|
| 1 | KMUKM | X,XXX.00 |
| 2 | Faculty | X,XXX.00 |
| 3 | Penajaan (sponsor name) | X,XXX.00 |
| | **JUMLAH KESELURUHAN** | **XX,XXX.00** |

### B. Anggaran Perbelanjaan (Estimated Expenditure)
Itemized breakdown with unit costs:

| No. | Item | Unit Cost (RM) | Quantity | Days | Total (RM) |
|-----|------|----------------|----------|------|------------|
| 1 | Jamuan (tengah hari) | 10.00 | 25 | 12 | 3,000.00 |
| | **JUMLAH KESELURUHAN** | | | | **3,000.00** |

> Revenue must equal or exceed expenditure. The "Days" column is used for multi-day programs where costs recur daily.

---

## Common Compliance Mistakes

1. **Exceeding pekeliling rate ceilings** — Jamuan rates MUST comply with Pekeliling Bendahari Bil. 8/2022 (as amended by Bil. 4/2025). Check [`04-perbelanjaan/jamuan/`](../04-perbelanjaan/jamuan/) for current rates.
2. **Vague line items** — "Lain-lain" or "Rencam" should be broken down into specifics.
3. **Missing per-unit breakdown** — Every item needs unit cost × quantity. Lump sums get rejected.
4. **Revenue-expenditure mismatch** — If you listed KMUKM as a revenue source, the KMUKM-funded items must match the amount approved.
5. **Including items not eligible for Pendahuluan** — Entertainment (hiburan), inventory/assets, and items requiring Pesanan Rasmi cannot be claimed via Pendahuluan.
