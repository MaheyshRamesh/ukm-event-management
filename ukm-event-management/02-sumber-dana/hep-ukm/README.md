## Overview

HEP-UKM (Hal Ehwal Pelajar) is the university-level student affairs department that:
- Provides direct peruntukan (allocation) for student programs
- Manages iSTAR approvals
- Processes Pesanan Rasmi through the eP@UKM system
- Handles Pendahuluan disbursements

## Key Role in Financial Flow

Even if your funding comes from Faculty or KMUKM, HEP-UKM is involved in:
1. **iSTAR approval** — All programs must be registered and approved in iSTAR
2. **Pesanan Rasmi processing** — HEP-UKM handles the eP@UKM broadcast to vendors
3. **Borang Pink (jamuan)** — Collected at HEP-UKM office
4. **Laporan Kewangan** — Financial reports are submitted through HEP-UKM or Portal Pelajar Bendahari
