## How to Use These Materials

These are **examples from a specific club (PERTAMA FTSM)**, not universal UKM templates. They're included to show what a good kewangan training module looks like and to give other clubs a head start.

When adapting for your own club:

1. **Verify all procedures** against the source documents in `01-garis-panduan/` and `02-sumber-dana/` — pekeliling and procedures change periodically.
2. **Replace club-specific info** (e.g., PERTAMA's internal loan process, FTSM faculty procedures) with your own faculty/club equivalents.
3. **Keep the flowcharts** — the procedural flows (pendahuluan, Pesanan Rasmi, jamuan, laporan kewangan) are universal UKM processes.
4. **Update contact info** — UKPEL contacts, faculty HEP contacts, and venue coordinators change across sessions.
