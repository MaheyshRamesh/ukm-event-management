# Kadar Jamuan (Food & Beverage Rates)

Official UKM rate ceilings for jamuan, as per **Pekeliling Bendahari Bil. 8/2022** and **Pindaan Bil. 4/2025**.

> ⚠️ These are **maximum rates**. You should negotiate with vendors within these ceilings. Exceeding these rates will cause your budget or claims to be rejected.

---

## Jamuan Eligibility (Updated per Pindaan Bil. 4/2025)

| Scenario | Eligible For |
|----------|-------------|
| Program **> 6 hours** | Jamuan Ringan + Makan Tengah Hari |
| Program **< 6 hours** but crosses **12:30pm** | Jamuan Ringan **OR** Makan Tengah Hari (pick one) |
| Program involves **Pengerusi LPU / Ahli LPU / Naib Canselor / external guests** | Jamuan Ringan + Makan Tengah Hari |
| Program **< 6 hours** and ends before 12:30pm | Jamuan Ringan only |

> **Key change in Pindaan Bil. 4/2025:** The old rule "programs under 6 hours only get jamuan ringan" has been **DIBATALKAN** (cancelled). Now, any program crossing 12:30pm qualifies for a proper meal even if total duration is under 6 hours.

---

## Rate Reference

The specific RM per-pax rates for each meal category (makan tengah hari, jamuan ringan, makan malam, etc.) are defined in **Pekeliling Bendahari Bil. 8/2022**. The rates vary by:

- Type of meal (ringan vs. full meal)
- Category of attendee (student vs. staff vs. VIP/external guest)
- Whether it includes drinks

> **Important:** The Pekeliling Bil. 8/2022 full rate table is not reproduced here to avoid outdated info if rates change. Always refer to the latest version at [`01-garis-panduan/`](../../01-garis-panduan/) or the Jabatan Bendahari website (https://bendahari.ukm.my).

## Which Route: Borang Pink vs Pesanan Rasmi?

| Total Jamuan Cost | Route | Form | Process |
|-------------------|-------|------|---------|
| **Below RM500** | Borang Pink | Borang Pesanan Jamuan (UKM-SPKPPP-PP04-BO02) | Fill form → PTj signature → submit to Kewangan HEP-UKM with supporting docs |
| **RM500 and above** | Pesanan Rasmi | Surat Permohonan Pesanan Rasmi + 4 supporting docs | Submit hardcopy to HEP-UKM → eP@UKM broadcast → vendor selection → PO issued |

---

## Required Documents for Each Route

### Borang Pink (< RM500)
1. Borang Pesanan Jamuan (UKM-SPKPPP-PP04-BO02) — signed by PTj (Penasihat)
2. Kertas Kerja iSTAR
3. Surat Kelulusan iSTAR
4. Surat Kelulusan Dana
5. Senarai nama peserta (matching the pax count requested)

> No quotation (sebut harga) required for Borang Pink, but you must have an agreement with the vendor on pricing.

### Pesanan Rasmi (≥ RM500)
1. Surat Permohonan Pesanan Rasmi
2. Kertas Kerja iSTAR
3. Surat Kelulusan iSTAR
4. Surat Kelulusan Dana
5. Sebut harga (quotation) from vendor
6. Senarai nama peserta

## Rules About In-Campus Jamuan

- **Must use UKM-registered food vendors.** You cannot order from random restaurants for in-campus events.
- **Cannot claim jamuan via Pendahuluan** for in-campus events. Jamuan must go through Borang Pesanan Jamuan or Pesanan Rasmi.
- **Outside-campus events:** Pendahuluan IS allowed for food. Provide details of pax count, menu, and rates before pendahuluan is approved.
