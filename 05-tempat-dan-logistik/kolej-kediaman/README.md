# Kolej Kediaman — Venue Booking

> ⚠️ **Placeholder** — Specific kolej kediaman booking procedures will be added as they are documented. Each kolej may have slightly different processes.

## General Notes

- **Kolej Pendeta Za'ba (KPZ)** and other kolej kediaman may offer hall spaces and facilities for student programs, especially overnight or multi-day events.
- Booking typically goes through the kolej's Pejabat Pengetua or HEP representative.
- Like all UKM venues, your program must be registered in iSTAR first.
- If your kolej has a specific booking procedure, consider contributing it to this repo (see [`CONTRIBUTING.md`](../../CONTRIBUTING.md)).
